Privacy & Terms

How Grammerly handles your data, and what you agree to when you use the bot.

Last updated: May 15, 2026
Privacy Policy Terms of Service
Short version: Grammerly only stores your Discord user ID and the number of times you have been corrected. It does not read, store, or share the content of your messages.

1.Information We Collect

Grammerly collects only the minimum data necessary to operate. Specifically, we collect and store:

  • Discord User ID - A numeric identifier assigned by Discord to your account. We store this to track how many times each user has been corrected.
  • Correction Count - The total number of times the bot has corrected a given user in the monitored channel. This is a plain integer counter stored per User ID.

We do not collect or store any of the following:

  • The content of any message you send
  • Your username, display name, avatar, or any other profile information
  • Your email address or any contact information
  • IP addresses, device identifiers, or browser data
  • Any data from channels other than the designated monitoring channel

2.How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used solely to power the bot's core features:

  • To respond to correction count requests with accurate numbers
  • To maintain a persistent record across bot restarts so counts are not lost

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the bot.

3.Data Storage and Security

Correction data is stored in a local database on the server where Grammerly is hosted. The data is not transmitted to any third party, cloud database, or external analytics service.

Access to this data is limited to the server operator. While we take reasonable precautions, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of data stored on self-hosted infrastructure.

4.Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to third parties. The only external service that Grammerly interacts with is Discord's API, which is necessary for the bot to function. Grammerly's use of the Discord API is governed by Discord's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

Spell-checking is performed entirely on the hosting server. No message content is sent to any external service during this process.

5.Data Retention

Correction counts associated with a Discord User ID are retained indefinitely as long as the bot remains deployed. There is no automatic expiry of this data.

If you wish to have your correction count removed, you may contact the server administrator and request deletion. Server administrators can remove individual records from the database on request.

6.Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights including:

  • Access - You may request to know what data is stored about your User ID.
  • Deletion - You may request that your correction count data be deleted.
  • Correction - If your data is inaccurate, you may request a correction.

To exercise any of these rights, contact the operator of the server where Grammerly is deployed. Since Grammerly is self-hosted software, the data controller is the individual or organization running the bot, not the bot's developer.

7.Children's Privacy

Grammerly is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). Discord itself requires users to be at least 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone below the minimum age required to use Discord. If you believe a minor's data has been collected, please contact the server administrator to have it removed.

8.Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects when the most recent changes were made. Continued use of Grammerly after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

9.Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please reach out through our Discord support server or submit a request to the server administrator.
